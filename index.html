(ME) ? "Who Am I" : "Where I came from"

My full name is Fungai Marshall Mangondoza, born and raised in Zimbabwe, a country in Southern Africa. This is where I spent most of my junior studies. In 2009 I was enrolled at Cyprus International University in Cyprus, as the name implies. In my second year I transferred to Eastern Mediterranean University also in Cyprus. On the 17th of February 2015, I managed to graduate with a Bsc in Management Information Systems, having a CGPA of 2.9/4.

During my studies, I was introduced to web development at Innovia Digital as an intern. My primary role was assist with code snippets in between projects and handle customers who has small requests. This gave me an opportunity to review senior dev code while I was at university. This made a difference in my career goals and what I wanted to achieve

My official professional path started immediately graduation after getting an opening as a full time web developer at a Digital Agency called Ata Bilisim. The company gave the developer full control over a project from design to deployment, and for 3 years, my experience and skills as a professional web developer increased. At first made code directly into themes for clients but dug deeper into custom wordpress plugins as client needs grew.

After 4 years, at my previous job. I moved to Nethouse Networks. The biggest internet service provider in North Cyprus. The journey has been exciting. I managed to create 3 online dashboards, 3 mobile apps both for ios and android and contribute to the team effort.
